Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about donating and requesting milk.

Is donor human milk safe for my baby?

Yes. All donated milk goes through health screening, Holder pasteurization, and laboratory testing before it is approved for distribution. Every batch is tracked from donor to recipient.

Who can donate milk?

Healthy, breastfeeding mothers who pass a medical and lifestyle screening may qualify as donors. Visit our Donate Milk page for the full eligibility criteria.

Who is eligible to receive milk?

Priority is given to premature infants, NICU patients, and babies whose mothers are medically unable to produce enough milk. Requests are reviewed and approved by our staff.

Is there a cost to receive milk?

Our milk bank operates on a non-profit basis. Any associated processing fees, if applicable, will be discussed during the request approval process.

How long does the donation or request process take?

It typically takes 1 or 2 weeks. Once approved, our staff will coordinate pickup (for donors) or pasteurized milk release (for recipients) as quickly as possible.

How to Become a Donor or Request for Milk

If You Are a Donor:

  1. Visit the "Donate Milk" Page Navigate to the milk bank portal to begin your registration as a mother-donor.
  2. Select your Donor Program Category Choose between Supsup Todo (Community-Based), Milky-Way (Hospital-Based), or Mom's Act (Household-Based).
  3. Fill in your Profile & Communication Details Enter your personal information, address, and preferred call schedule for your interview.
  4. Submit your application Confirm your details and send in your application.

If You Are Requesting Milk:

  1. Visit the "Request Milk" Page Navigate to the beneficiary registration portal.
  2. Fill in Beneficiary and Medical Details Provide the patient or infant's information alongside the required medical details.
  3. Upload Required Documents Attach necessary documents such as Prescriptions and a Valid ID.
  4. Submit your Request Finalize and submit the completed application for review.
